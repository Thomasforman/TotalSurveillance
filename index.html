Most of what is considered normal in our society are just unrealistic expectations that cycle through our media. Our notes that we take about our own actions or feelings deeply reflect how we feel we fit into society. Often the gaze of others affects how we regulate ourselves. A lot of these insecurities and tendencies to self-surveil come out when we are writing in our journals.
